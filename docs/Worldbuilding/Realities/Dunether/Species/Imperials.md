# Imperials

    Article contains spoilers: Full Series

Dunether is populated by the Imperials, a sort of elf-like species and where the Hastors originated.
Despite their name, they don’t have grand buildings like you’d see in a LOTR movie. Architecture here is rather more of a grab bag. Being one of the first realities, it was a melting pot for all of the then-young original Immortals’ ideas and therefore has a lot of random parts all molded together.
