Mortal souls are the fuel of the cosmos. The nature of this fuel is to make things change, to push things outside of their regular patterns. This keeps the cosmos interesting and therefore worthwhile for the Narrator to continue keeping alive.

Immortal souls are dedicated to continuing the cycle of the cosmos. They want everything to stay pristine.

Because of their nature, both types of souls are bound to come into conflict, and therefore provide interest to the cosmos.

Without the idea's existence on the higher narrative plane, this world ceases to exist. Human memories and ideas count, so does digital storage such as this. If all of it is eviscerated, then the cosmos will unexist on the spot.
